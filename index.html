Benefits of FLEXXI Team

Easy Registration

Download the free FLEXXI Team App and fill out your profile. We review and approve your documents within 48 hours.

Jobs near you

FLEXXI shows you orders that are available near you so that you don't have to work far from home.

Flexible schedule

You have no obligations. Work when you are free and feel like it. You are your own boss!

Immediate and secure payment

All payments are secured by a direct payment system and your income is transferred immediately after completion of the service. You don't have to wait for the monthly payroll anymore.

User-friendly interface

The FLEXXI Team app is easy to use, and you don't need to be tech-savvy. Just check the available jobs near you and select the one that suits your preferences and schedule.

Find out about your tasks in advance

Before you accept the job, find out what services are expected of you, where the job will be performed, and how much you will be paid. If you still have questions, you can always chat directly with the client.
